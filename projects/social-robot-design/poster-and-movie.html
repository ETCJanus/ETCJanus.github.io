Reflection

I was the facilitator for the movie and made the script beforehand, this helped structure while filming. Although we deviated from it a bit afterwards, it helped understand what we wanted, like a scene where we describe the problem first. What I would change about the video is putting more time in more professional shots or a better way to shoot the scenes. It was more an impromptu moment where we just did it, and I would rather have a reconstruction we acted out a bit better, showcasing how the toolkit would work more clearly.
