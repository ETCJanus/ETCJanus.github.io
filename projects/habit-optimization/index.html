Quantified Self Tracker

Owner Login

A personal web app I made to track my daily routines, using a GitHub-style contribution grid to visualize how I'm doing over months or years at a glance.

I built this because I was tired of using habit trackers that were either full of ads or locked basic features behind subscriptions. I just wanted a clean, fast way to log my days and see a dense overview of my consistency without any extra noise. It's not meant to be a product, just my own deeply personal dashboard.
